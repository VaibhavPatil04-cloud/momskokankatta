# Mom's Kokan Katta - Website Design & Structure

Based on a deep analysis for the , here is a comprehensive breakdown of the design system, structure, and styling of the **Mom's Kokan Katta** website. 

The website exudes a **premium, elegant, and sophisticated** fine-dining vibe. The design is modern, relying heavily on high-quality imagery, a rich color palette, and classic typography.

---

## 1. Overall Design System & Styling

* **Color Palette:**
    * **Primary Backgrounds:** Deep Charcoal/Soft Black (creates a premium, intimate evening vibe) and Off-White/Cream (used to break up sections and provide breathing room).
    * **Accent Color:** Gold / Muted Mustard Yellow. This is the signature color used for all Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, icons, active link underlines, and decorative dividers.
    * **Secondary Backgrounds:** Earthy tones like muted sage green/olive are used for CTA banners at the bottom of pages.
* **Typography:**
    * **Headings:** A classic, high-contrast **Serif font** (similar to *Playfair Display* or *Cinzel*). It gives a luxurious and vintage yet modern feel.
    * **Body Text:** A clean, geometric **Sans-Serif font** (similar to *Montserrat* or *Poppins*). It ensures high readability for descriptions and functional text.
* **UI Elements:**
    * **Buttons:** Sharp, rectangular buttons. Primary buttons have a solid gold background with dark text. Secondary buttons are transparent with gold borders.
    * **Icons:** Minimalist, thin-line icons in gold.
    * **Images:** Edge-to-edge backgrounds with dark overlays to make the white/gold text pop. Also uses masonry grids for galleries.

---

## 2. Global Elements (Present across all pages)

* **Header (Navbar):**
    * **Left:** Brand Logo (Elegant "Larive" typography with a subtle leaf motif on the 'v', and "Kitchen and Cocktail" subtext).
    * **Center/Right:** Navigation links (Home, About Us, Menu, Gallery, Contact Us, Reserve a Table). Active links are highlighted in gold.
    * **Far Right:** A prominent gold "ORDER ONLINE" button.
* **Footer (Dark Theme):**
    * **Left Column:** Quick links (About Us, Menus, Gallery, Contact Us).
    * **Center Column:** Centralized logo, precise address (2nd Floor, Viraj Vertex, College Road, Nashik), operational hours (12:00 PM - 11:00 PM), and a "RESERVE A TABLE" button.
    * **Right Column:** Contact options with icons (WhatsApp, Instagram, Call Now, Email).
    * **Bottom Bar:** Copyright info, credit line ("Made with ❤️ in Nashik By MorphX Solutions"), and a "Scroll to Top" circular arrow button.

---

## 3. Page-by-Page Structure Breakdown

### A. Home Page
* **Hero Slider Section:** Full-screen carousel. Features 3 distinct slides showcasing Ambiance, Food, and Vibe. 
    * *Structure:* Large Serif Heading -> Sans-serif subheading -> Gold CTA Button. Features slider dots (bottom left) and navigation arrows (bottom right).
* **Quick Reservation Form:** A dark, sleek card allowing users to book directly from the home page.
* **"Our Strength" Section:** * *Structure:* Dark background, centered heading. A 4-column grid featuring gold icons and short text (Hygienic Food, Fresh Environment, Skilled Chefs, Events & Gatherings).
* **Highlights Section:** * *Structure:* Three vertical image panels side-by-side. Each acts as a card with a background image, darkened overlay, and text detailing "Curated Menu", "Private Celebrations", and "Signature Drinks".
* **Video/Testimonial Section:** "The Larive Experience". Features a background image with a prominent Play button icon in the center, accompanied by an elegant quote by Virginia Woolf.

### B. About Us Page
* **Hero Section:** Image background with the title "Above the Ordinary".
* **Our Story Section:** Dark background with a very subtle, oversized watermark of a leaf. Text explains the philosophy ("Where Flavour Meets Soul").
* **Visual Collage:** "Crafted Moments Every Time". An asymmetrical, masonry-style layout of 3-4 images showing the interior and food.
* **Heritage Section:** A split layout (or stacked on mobile) featuring a high-quality image of the restaurant's exterior alongside text detailing the design inspiration.
* **Pre-Footer CTA:** Sage green background block encouraging users to "Discover Our Story" with buttons to Reserve a Table or Contact Us.

### C. Menu Page
* **Hero Section:** Title "Our Menu" over a dark, blurred background.
* **Intro Section:** Text block "Crafting Culinary Excellence" followed by three quick feature points (Vegetarian Delights, Craft Cocktails, Terrace Experience).
* **Menu Navigation Cards:** * *Structure:* Two large, distinct cards. One for "Food Menu" and one for "Drinks Menu". Each features a large circular icon at the top, a title, a short description, and a full-width gold button.
* **Pre-Footer CTA:** "Experience Larive's Culinary Journey" on a light greenish background.

### D. Gallery Page
* **Hero Section:** Title "Gallery" with a brief intro text.
* **Filterable Grid Section:**
    * *Structure:* Horizontal category tabs (All, Ambiance, Cuisine, Drinks, Events). Active tab text is gold.
    * *Content:* A clean, spacing-optimized grid of high-resolution images showcasing food, drinks, and the venue.
* **Pre-Footer CTA:** "See Larive in Action" banner.

### E. Reserve a Table Page
* **Hero Section:** Title "Reserve Your Table".
* **Main Booking Form:** * *Structure:* A dark container over a light background. 2-column input fields (Name, Email, WhatsApp, Persons, Date, Time, Special Requests) with a full-width gold "BOOK A TABLE" button.
* **"How It Works" Section:** * *Structure:* Light background. A 4-step process displayed in distinct white cards with gold circular number badges (1. Fill Out the Form, 2. Confirmation, 3. Pay Token, 4. Table Reserved).
* **Important Notice Box:** A highlighted rectangular box with a soft yellow/gold border detailing the cancellation/rescheduling policy and contact numbers.
* **Pre-Footer CTA:** "Book Your Perfect Table".

### F. Contact Us Page
* **Hero Section:** Title "We're Here to Connect".
* **Contact Info Grid:** * *Structure:* Off-white background. Four clean white cards with subtle shadows. Each contains a gold icon, title, and detail (Address with 'Get Directions' button, Phone with 'Call Now' button, Email, Opening Hours).
* **Message Form Section:** * *Structure:* Split layout (stacked on mobile). The left side features a sleek contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Subject, Message) on a dark grey card. The right side contains welcoming text ("We'd Love to Hear from You").

---

## Summary
The website is a textbook example of modern luxury hospitality UI. It uses the **Dark Mode aesthetic** to create a feeling of exclusivity and nighttime dining, while strategically using lighter sections to make content like forms and contact details highly legible. The user flow is highly optimized, placing "Reserve a Table" calls-to-action on almost every single scroll.
